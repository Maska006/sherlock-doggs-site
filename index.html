About Me

My name is Sherlock Doggs, a private investigator with an exceptional nose and a nose for trouble. From missing socks to stolen tuna sandwiches, no mystery is too small — or too strange — for me to solve. When others give up, I dig in (literally, sometimes).

  • Master of Observation: I sit. I stare. I know everything.
